eQuran Library : Comprehensive Online Quran & Hadith Platform

eQuran Library is a comprehensive online Quran and Hadith platform launched in December 2018 by Usman Pervez, offering users seamless access to the full Arabic text of the Quran alongside a rich array of Urdu and English translations, word‑by‑word exegesis, classical tafaseer (commentaries), and authentic hadith collections in a clean, mobile‑friendly interface.

The site’s mission is to make understanding and sharing the teachings of the Quran and Sunnah easy and accessible, reflecting the founder’s conviction that true success in this world and the hereafter comes through adherence to these sources. Visitors can recite by Surah or by Ruku, choose from multiple translators and tafseer scholars, and track their last‑read pages—all within a responsive web design that adapts to desktop and mobile browsers. For on‑the‑go study, eQuran Library also offers an official Android app with over 16 000 downloads, a 4.6‑star rating on Google Play, and zero ads or data collection, making it one of the more lightweight and privacy‑friendly Quran apps available.

Background & Mission

eQuran Library was conceptualized and developed by Usman Pervez in Rawalpindi, Pakistan, and went live in December 2018 to fulfill the responsibility Muslims have to understand, practice, and disseminate the teachings of the Quran and Sunnah. The “Taste the sweetness of Imaan” tagline encapsulates its goal of nurturing faith through knowledge, and the About Us statement underscores the creator’s prayer that this endeavor boosts both individual and communal spiritual growth.

Core Features

Quranic Recitation

  • Recite by Surah or Ruku: Users can select any of the 114 Surahs or navigate by Ruku (sections) for targeted recitation.

Translation Collection

  • Urdu & English Translations: Hosts four Urdu translations (Maududi, Jalandhary, Junagarhi, Taqi Usmani) and two English renditions (Saheeh International, Maududi).

Tafseer (Commentaries)

  • Extensive Tafseer Library: Over a dozen classical and contemporary commentaries—including Tafseer Ibn-e-Kaseer, Tafheem-ul-Quran, Maarif-ul-Quran, Ahsan‑ul‑Bayan, and Tadabbur-e-Quran—provide deep contextual insights.

Word‑by‑Word Analysis

  • Bilingual Word‑for‑Word: Features Nazar Ahmed and Farhat Hashmi’s breakdowns for Surah‑level and Ayah‑level studies in Urdu and English, aiding vocabulary building and precise comprehension.

Hadith Collections

  • Authentic Ahadith: Curates key Sunni collections—Sahih Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, Sunan Abu Dawood, Sunan An‑Nasai, Tirmidhi, Ibn Majah, Mishkaat Shareef, Muwatta Imam Malik, Musnad Ahmad, Maarif‑ul‑Hadith, Riyad‑us‑Saliheen—for seamless hadith lookup alongside Quranic study.

Mobile Experience

The official eQuran Library Android app, published by UsmanPervez, bundles the website’s full feature set into a native interface with offline bookmarking and last‑read tracking. Rated 4.6 / 5 on Google Play with around 16 000 installs, the app is lauded for its light footprint (~2.7 MB) and privacy‑focused design—no ads, no data collection.

Accessibility & Site Performance

With a fully responsive design, the web interface adapts fluidly across screen sizes, offering font-size controls and a clear navigation menu for each content category. While its SEO presence is still growing, the site demonstrates solid performance and room for international visibility expansion.

Conclusion

eQuran Library stands out as a holistic digital hub for Quranic and Hadith studies, marrying classical scholarship with modern web and mobile accessibility. Its rich translations, comprehensive commentaries, user‑friendly recitation tools, and privacy‑centric mobile app make it ideal for both serious students and casual readers, all in an intuitive, shareable format.
