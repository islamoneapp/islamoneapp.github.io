Quran Word by Word Translations (Urdu & English)

Multicolor, easy to read word by word translation of Quran in urdu and English. Best for memorizing translations.

Search in Quran, Translation and Tafseer

With IslamOne's advanced Search feature, you can find the exact or partial match of what you are looking for in the Quran, Translations and Tafaseer. In addition you can jump to any specific verse in any surah.

Urdu and English Tafaseer Collection

IslamOne offers a completely free and a visually rich, curated bookstore where you can find 9 Urdu Tafaseer and 2 English Tafaseer on the go. With our manual/ auto resume feature, you can continue reading where you left. You can also save bookmarks and add personalized notes. IslamOne is thoughtfully designed for peaceful, distraction-free reading with no ads in any part of the application.

Fiqh & Seerah Muhammad SAW

Complete Seerat-un Nabi SAW, showcasing the biography of Prophet Muhammad SAW and 33 of his(SAW) companions compiled by Abdullah Farani is also available for free.

Azkaar & Dua Collection

IslamOne offers a pool of daily azkaar, day and night prayers, daily sunnahs, 6 kalmay and much more. Very useful for kids to learn short duaas.

Authentic Hadith Collection

IslamOne offers a completely free and a visually rich, curated Hadith bookstore with 11 hadith books on the go. IslamOne is thoughtfully designed for peaceful, distraction-free reading with no ads in any part of the application.
