What does it look like?

IslamOne is an all-in-one comprehensive Quran app with Free Islamic content for everyone. The idea behind IslamOne is to provide Free Islamic education to everyone without needing to buy the app or getting annoyed by inappropriate ads while browsing through Quran. IslamOne is available for both android and iOS devices.

You get a pool of Islamic content with a single download featuring:

  • Homepage featuring Ayah, Dua, Word and Hadith of the moment.
  • A simple customizable interface with multiple font options.
  • Light and dark mode.
  • User- friendly and efficient Settings menu.
  • Auto Resume feature helps you continue reading exactly from where you left.
  • Search in Quran, Translation and Tafseer.
